about the band

[NOTE: THIS TEXT WAS BORROWED FROM https://vanderbilthustler.com/2022/04/08/vanderbilt-band-edgehill-rocks-exit-in/ AND IS ONLY HERE AS A PLACEHOLDER] Edgehill is a Nashville based alternative rock group consisting of Chris Kelly (lead vocals / guitar), Jake Zimmermann (lead guitar / vocals), Andrei Olaru (bass) and Aidan Cunningham (drums). They formed in the fall of 2021, when Chris & Jake met at a TMR jam session and started writing their debut single “Shooting Glances”, which amassed over 450k streams in its first 6 months since release. Soon afterwards they started playing local house shows before graduating to esteemed venues such as EXIT/IN and Basement East. Edgehill is known for their energetic live performances and engaging social media presence, with Kelly’s TikTok racking up several videos with 1M+ views. This recognition has afforded them incredible opportunities to play alongside artists such as Stephen Sanchez, Jordy Searcy, Charlie Burg, Dayglow, and more. Edgehill is currently in the studio recording their debut project.
